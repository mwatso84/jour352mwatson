How are college students faring?

Findings show mental health concerns still a large issue on campuses

By Matthew Watson

Recent surveys from the American College Health Association Gallup suggest that undergraduate students on a massive scale are struggling with mental health. Over 75% of students are experiencing moderate to serious psychological distress, and more than half have been diagnosed with either anxiety or depression.
